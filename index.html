About me

I'm in love with creating feeling. I'll help bring your project to life.

I graduated from Macalester College with a degree in Computer Science and Music in 2017. Since then, I've worked at Fjorge fulltime as a Front End Developer. As an animator, I've worked to create vivid animations for the likes of Yussef Dayes of Yussef Kamaal, Clever Austin (drummer of Australian future-jazz group Hiatus Kaiyote), Shlohmo, Redbull x Wax Poetics, Swarvy, record labels Friends of Friends, Radio Juicy , Paxico Records and many others. If you're interested in animated album art, custom motion graphics, sound design or web development, please get in touch and we'll make something happen!
